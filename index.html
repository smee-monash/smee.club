The Society of Monash Electrical Engineers

We are a student-run club aimed to bring together students pursuing studies of Electrical and Computer Systems Engineering (ECSE) at Monash University Clayton.

What we're about

SMEE aims to bridge the gap between emerging graduates and industry professionals. Throughout the year we host careers events allowing members to learn more about our sponsors and career pathways. SMEE gives students the chance to share knowledge and experiences between one another. This includes connecting students to the right people to help and answer queries in regards to vacation employment, graduate opportunities and industry practices.

The club also offers industry networking and seminars targeted specifically towards fields relating to Electrical and Computer Systems Engineering, such as software development, communication systems and power related careers. Club memberships are open to anyone who is curious about the world of Electrical Engineering or who simply wants the odd free lunch and drinks with friendly students who share similar interests.

Keep in contact

Whether you are a new student or industry professional, we're always happy to hear feedback, answer questions, and discuss future collaborative opportunities. Feel free to send us a message and we will endeavor to reply as soon as we can.
